Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 2024

1. Information Collection

We collect information you provide directly to us through this portal, including inquiries via contact forms and registration for GAD-related events. This may include your name, email address, and contact details.

2. Use of Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Provide updates on GAD projects and programs.
  • Respond to your requests for information or assistance.
  • Improve the functionality and content of this website.

3. Data Protection

In accordance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173), the Municipality of Montalban implements strict security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

Contact Our Data Officer

If you have questions regarding your data, please contact us at privacy@montalban.gov.ph
