OUR GUIDING PRINCIPLES

The foundational framework driving our commitment to gender equality and inclusive development.

Our Vision

A community where women, men, girl-boy children and persons with disabilities enjoy equal rights, opportunities and a high quality of life.

Our Mission

Commitment to institutionalize the mainstreaming of GAD advocacy in the development process and ensuring the facilitation of activities that highlights the importance of gender equality, not only as a fundamental human right, but also as essential to poverty reduction, improved living standards, sustainable economic growth, and effective and accountable governance.

Our Mandate

To develop and implement a gender responsive plan, programs, projects and activities (PPAs) that is geared towards the provision of equal opportunity to women and men, girl-boy children and persons with disability; as well as ensure the promotion/advocacy of the women and children’s rights and interest in the most effective and efficient manner.

The GAD Pillars

Inclusivity

Ensuring every citizen has a seat at the table regardless of gender, age, or physical ability.

Equality

Upholding rights and opportunities as fundamental human rights for all municipal sectors.

Sustainability

Driving economic growth and effective governance through gender-responsive resource allocation.
